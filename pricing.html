How are "interactions" defined and counted?

An interaction is defined as an exchange between your AI agent and a customer or prospect. This includes email messages, chat messages, or voice conversations. Each back-and-forth counts as one interaction. For example, if a customer asks a question and the AI agent responds, that counts as one interaction.

Can I change plans as my business grows?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your plan at any time as your needs evolve. When upgrading, we'll prorate your billing to make the transition seamless. If you need to downgrade, you can do so at the end of your current billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my monthly interaction limit?

If you exceed your monthly interaction limit, you'll be charged for additional interactions at a discounted rate. We'll notify you as you approach your limit so you can decide whether to upgrade to a higher plan or pay for additional interactions. Your AI agents will continue to function without interruption.

Is onboarding included in all plans?

Yes, all plans include onboarding support to get you started. The Starter plan includes standard onboarding with setup assistance and basic training. Professional and Enterprise plans include more comprehensive onboarding with personalized configuration, integration support, and team training sessions. For more details, visit our Onboarding page.

Do you offer custom pricing for specific industries?

Yes, we offer specialized pricing for certain industries such as healthcare, financial services, and education. These packages include industry-specific AI training, compliance features, and relevant integrations. Contact our sales team to discuss your industry requirements and get a custom quote.

How long does implementation typically take?

Our standard implementation timeline is 2-4 weeks, depending on your plan and specific requirements. Starter plans can be implemented in as little as 1-2 weeks, while Enterprise implementations with custom integrations and training may take 3-4 weeks. Our onboarding team will provide you with a detailed timeline during the kickoff call.
