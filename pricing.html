Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How does the conversation limit work?

A conversation is defined as a complete interaction with a prospect, from initial outreach to final resolution. This can include multiple messages back and forth. We don't count individual messages, only complete conversation threads. If you're approaching your limit, we'll notify you so you can upgrade if needed.

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes! You can upgrade your plan at any time, and the changes will take effect immediately. If you upgrade mid-billing cycle, we'll prorate the cost. Downgrading is also possible but will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What kind of support do you offer?

All plans include standard support via email with a 24-hour response time. Growth plans and above include priority support with faster response times. Enterprise plans get dedicated account managers and technical support with response times guaranteed by SLA.

Do you offer a free trial?

We don't offer a traditional free trial, but we do provide a comprehensive demo of the platform tailored to your specific use case. This allows you to see exactly how ApexSalesAI would work for your business before making a commitment.

What integrations do you support?

We integrate with all major CRM systems including Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and more. We also support email platforms like Gmail and Outlook, and communication tools like Slack and Microsoft Teams. Enterprise plans include custom integrations to fit your specific tech stack.
