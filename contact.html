  • Enterprise-grade encryption: AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Robust access controls: Role-based access control (RBAC), multi-factor authentication (MFA), and detailed audit logging
  • Regular security assessments: Penetration testing, vulnerability scanning, and code reviews
  • Secure cloud infrastructure: Hosted in SOC 2 compliant data centers with 99.9% uptime

We also offer data residency options for clients with specific geographic requirements and can provide additional security documentation upon request under NDA.

How does AI agent training work?

ApexSalesAI agents undergo a comprehensive training process to ensure they represent your brand effectively:

  1. Knowledge base integration: We ingest your product documentation, sales scripts, FAQs, and support knowledge base
  2. Conversation analysis: Our system analyzes successful sales and support interactions from your existing data
  3. Workflow mapping: We configure agents to follow your specific business processes and playbooks
  4. Supervised learning: Initial interactions are closely monitored with human feedback
  5. Continuous improvement: Agents learn from ongoing interactions and feedback to improve performance

The training process typically takes 2-3 weeks, with agents becoming increasingly effective over time as they learn from real-world interactions. Our platform includes a management dashboard where you can review agent performance, provide feedback, and adjust settings as needed.
