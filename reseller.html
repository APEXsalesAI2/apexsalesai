Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our partner program

What are the requirements to become a partner?

Our partner program is designed to be accessible to a variety of technology providers. Basic requirements include an established business with a focus on B2B technology solutions, a dedicated sales team, and a commitment to completing our certification program. Higher tiers have additional requirements including revenue commitments and dedicated technical resources.

How long does the onboarding process take?

Most partners complete our onboarding process within 2-3 weeks. This includes the application review (1-2 business days), partnership agreement completion (dependent on your legal team), and certification training (typically 5-10 hours). Our goal is to get you selling as quickly as possible while ensuring you have the knowledge and resources to be successful.

Can we white-label the ApexSalesAI platform?

Yes, Gold and Platinum partners have white-labeling capabilities. This includes the ability to customize the platform interface with your branding, use your own domain, and present the solution as part of your technology stack. We provide comprehensive white-labeling guidelines and support to ensure a seamless brand experience for your clients.

What kind of partner support do you provide?

Our partner support varies by tier but includes resources such as a dedicated Partner Portal with sales and marketing materials, regular product updates, technical documentation, and training resources. Gold and Platinum partners receive dedicated Partner Success Managers who provide strategic guidance, deal support, and assistance with client implementations. All partners have access to our technical support team for client-specific questions.

How are partner payments handled?

We offer flexible payment models based on your preference. Most partners choose our monthly commission structure where we manage the billing relationship with the end client and pay you your margin monthly. For white-label partners, we also offer a wholesale model where you purchase licenses at the discounted partner rate and handle client billing directly. Payments are processed within 30 days of the end of each month.
